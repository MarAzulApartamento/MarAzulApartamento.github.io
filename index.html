FAQ

How many people can the apartment accommodate?

Our cozy apartment comfortably hosts between 2 to 4 guests, making it perfect for a small family, a group of friends, or even a couple or a few singles looking for a relaxing getaway.

Where is it located?

The apartment is ideally situated at R. Dom Luís da Silveira 8600-575 Lagos, Portugal , offering easy access to all the local attractions and the beauty of the Algarve region.

Are pets allowed?

Absolutely! Your furry friends are more than welcome to stay with us. 🐾

Is there Wi-Fi?

Yes, we offer high-speed Wi-Fi, so you can stay connected whether you're working or streaming your favorite shows

Is parking available?

Conveniently, there is free parking just a minute's walk from the apartment, making it easy for you to explore the area without any hassle.

Are linens and towels included?

Yes, we provide fresh linens and towels for your comfort.

Is a crib and high chair available?

Of course! We offer both a crib and a high chair to make your stay even more convenient for families with young children.
